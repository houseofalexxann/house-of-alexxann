# QA checklist — before any claim goes public

- [ ] Every public claim carries ≥1 citation with a locator, **or** is
      explicitly labeled `synthesis` / `experimental`.
- [ ] Every quote verified verbatim against the edition cited.
- [ ] Historical claims name **which tradition and era** — never "astrology
      says".
- [ ] Nothing beyond fair-use excerpt length from in-copyright works appears
      publicly; `rights:` in source.yaml is respected.
- [ ] Tone check against the House philosophy: no fear-based framing, no
      deterministic prediction, no medical/legal/financial diagnosis; the
      reader leaves informed, empowered, curious.
- [ ] Translations/transliterations of Sanskrit and Greek terms are
      consistent with the existing glossary spellings.
- [ ] Epistemic status is the *narrowest* honest label (when in doubt between
      doctrine and scholarship, cite both readings separately).
